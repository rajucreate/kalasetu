About Kalasetu

Kalasetu is a cultural commerce platform that uplifts artisans while preserving tradition. Each product is reviewed for authenticity, ensuring fair trade, ethical sourcing, and visibility for underserved craft communities.

How It Works

1. Artisan Uploads

Artisans upload their handcrafted products with cultural context.

2. Authenticity Verified

Cultural consultants validate authenticity and heritage value.

3. Global Purchase

Buyers discover and purchase verified products worldwide.
